Who we are

A Trusted Voice
at the United Nations

The World Society of Victimology is a not-for-profit NGO holding Special Category consultative status with the UN Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) and the Council of Europe. Our worldwide membership includes researchers, practitioners, lawyers, social workers, and policy makers united by a common concern for victims.

What we do

Society Programmes

Four core programmes through which the WSV creates impact across the global victimology community.

🎓

International Symposium

The premier global event held every three years in a different country, attended by hundreds of academics and practitioners worldwide.

🌐

International Advocacy

Accredited representatives to the Council of Europe and UN offices in New York, Geneva, and Vienna to champion victims' rights.

📰

The Victimologist

Our newsletter published since 1982, keeping members informed on research, victim rights, and Society matters worldwide.

🤝

WSV Committees

Standing committees open to all members in good standing — from UN Liaison to Standards, Norms, and Communications.
